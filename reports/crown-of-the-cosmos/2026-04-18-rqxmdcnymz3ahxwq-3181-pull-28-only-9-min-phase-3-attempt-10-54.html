Simple strategy

Crown of the Cosmos Heroic is a three-phase fight against Alleria with two intermissions. Phase 3 is where almost every Heroic raid wipes: Aspect of the End tethers must be broken in the right order, and the raid must glide to the next platform slice before Devouring Cosmos covers the current one.

Fight plan

  • Phase 1: interrupt every Void Barrage cast. Heal through Voidstalker Sting; it drops off on its own.
  • Intermission 1 / Phase 2: burn the Rift Simulacrum's Cosmic Barrier. Ranged bait Void Expulsion puddles on the fissure edge.
  • Intermission 2: stand still in your assigned slice during the Orbiting Matter / 300% arrow window — moving is what kills people here.
  • Phase 3 (62%): Aspect tethers break in order Ranged → Melee → Tank, from 30+ yards apart.
  • Phase 3: pick up a cosmic feather EVERY time Devouring Cosmos is about to cover your slice and glide to the next one.
  • Phase 3: when Grasp of Emptiness anchors you, dodge the beam on expiration — move the whole group as a unit.
  • Do NOT dispel Null Corona. Heal through it. Dispel jumps the absorb to a new player.

Phase plan

Phase 1 — The Courtyard

Tank-split on Vorelus and Demair, interrupt adds, bait Void Expulsion away from the group.

Positioning: One tank solos Vorelus at its portal, the rest of the raid stacks on Demair. Ranged play slightly outside the stack to bait Void Expulsion puddles away.

Assignments: Fixed Void Barrage interrupt rotation on Undying Voidspawn — if a cast completes, the add becomes unkillable. Silverstrike Arrows hit each Sentinel once; do not double-hit or you reset stacks.

Movement: Drop Void Remnants pools away from melee. Move through Silverstrike Arrows to cleanse Sting if it is piling up.

Intermission 1 / Phase 2 — The Severed Rift

Break the Rift Simulacrum's Cosmic Barrier, manage Phase 2 ricocheting arrows, keep the group rotating along the fissure edge.

Positioning: Stack near the outer fissure boundary of your slice. Rotate counter-clockwise when puddles run out of space.

Assignments: Healers commit a raid cooldown for the Cosmic Barrier duration — it is a damage window, not healable ambient. Ranged continue baiting Void Expulsion near existing puddles to keep lanes clean.

Movement: Stay out of the ricochet path of Phase 2 Silverstrike Arrows. Finish the Barrier burn before Phase 3 opens at 62%.

Intermission 2 — Orbiting Matter

Stand still during the 300% arrow damage window and rotate slices to dodge Orbiting Matter bolts.

Positioning: Stay centered in your assigned platform slice. Rotate slice only when the Orbiting Matter pattern requires it.

Assignments: Healers pre-shield: moving into Orbiting Matter during the 300% debuff is lethal.

Movement: DO NOT move during the 300% arrow damage debuff window — that is the primary kill case here.

Phase 3 — The End of the End (62% → 0%)

Tether-break order, feather gliding, and Grasp dodging are the whole phase. Sting and raid damage are side effects.

Positioning: Raid stacks on Alleria by default. Aspect tether targets spread 30+ yards before breaking — Ranged first, then Melee, then Tank.

Assignments: Call the tether break order out loud each time: 'RANGED now, MELEE now, TANK now.' Assign a feather pickup target each Devouring Cosmos cycle — nobody should be standing in the covered slice. Healers DO NOT dispel Null Corona. Heal through it.

Movement: Grab a Dark Rush feather every Devouring Cosmos and glide to the next slice. Grasp of Emptiness: move as a group away from the beam lanes when the anchor expires. Aspect tether breaks happen from 30+ yards, never stacked.

Big mistakes to avoid

  • Breaking Aspect tethers simultaneously or in the wrong order — this stacks 300% physical damage and turns Gravity Collapse into a raid wipe.
  • Staying on a slice after Devouring Cosmos starts covering it — the slice becomes a no-heal zone.
  • Dispelling Null Corona — the absorb jumps to someone else and usually lands on a target with no cooldowns.
  • Moving during the Intermission 2 300% arrow damage debuff — arrows become lethal.
  • Letting Void Barrage casts complete in Phase 1/2 — adds become unkillable and deal massive damage.
  • Treating Voidstalker Sting as a critical kill mechanic — on Heroic it is healable. Spending CDs on Sting steals them from the real overlaps.
