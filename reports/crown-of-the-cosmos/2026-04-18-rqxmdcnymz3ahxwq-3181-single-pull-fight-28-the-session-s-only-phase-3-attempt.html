Simple strategy

Crown of the Cosmos is a 3-phase fight with two intermissions. The Rift Simulacrum shares health with the boss via Voidlink — when the Simulacrum dies at 62% boss HP, Phase 3 (The End of the End) begins. Heroic is a heavy HPS and DPS check; interrupts in P1/P2 and tether order in P3 are non-negotiable.

Fight plan

  • P1 (100%–~85% boss HP): hard-interrupt every Void Barrage on Undying Voidspawn. One arrow per Sentinel — never re-hit the same Sentinel. Drop Void Remnants on the edge.
  • Intermission 1 / P2 (Severed Rift): ranged bait Void Expulsion together on the fissure edge. Burn the Rift Simulacrum shield while a healer raid CD covers Cosmic Barrier. Rotate counter-clockwise along the fissure for Silverstrike Ricochet.
  • Intermission 2 (Orbiting Matter): stay centered in your assigned platform slice. Do not move during the 300% arrow damage debuff window.
  • P3 (from 62% boss HP): Ranged → Melee → Tank tether break order, 30+ yards apart. Grab feathers for Devouring Cosmos slice jumps. Reposition as a unit for Grasp of Emptiness / Bursting Emptiness.

Phase plan

Phase 1 (100% → ~85%)

Interrupt rotation phase. Add management with Silverstrike arrows.

Positioning: Raid in standard stack. Tanks keep boss centered.

Assignments: Three named interrupters per Undying Voidspawn, with a backup. One arrow per Sentinel — do not re-hit.

Movement: Drop Void Remnants on the room edge. Keep the center clear.

Intermission 1 / Phase 2 (Severed Rift)

DPS the Rift Simulacrum through Cosmic Barrier while healers raid-CD. Rotate for Ricochet.

Positioning: Ranged slightly outside melee stack to bait Void Expulsion. Bait together on the fissure edge.

Assignments: Healers pre-assign raid CDs for each Cosmic Barrier. DPS burn the shield.

Movement: Rotate counter-clockwise along the fissure edge as Silverstrike Ricochet space shrinks.

Intermission 2 (Orbiting Matter)

Platform slice transition. Do not move during the 300% arrow damage window.

Positioning: Each player locks their assigned slice. Stay centered.

Assignments: Call the slice per role before the intermission starts.

Movement: No movement during the debuff window. Rotate slice only to dodge incoming bolts.

Phase 3 — The End of the End (from 62% boss HP)

Tether break order is the whole fight. Feathers are required for slice jumps.

Positioning: Ranged 30+ yards from tether obelisk. Tanks take tether last. Group moves as a unit for Grasp.

Assignments: Ranged → Melee → Tank break order. One feather per player per Devouring Cosmos. Do not dispel Null Corona.

Movement: Jump to a fresh slice via feather before Devouring Cosmos covers the current slice. Reposition off obelisks before Grasp/Bursting Emptiness fires.

Big mistakes to avoid

  • Letting a Void Barrage cast complete. The add goes unkillable and the raid eats massive damage.
  • Re-hitting the same Sentinel with Silverstrike Arrow — it resets stacks.
  • Breaking Aspect of the End tethers out of order or too close. Each break stacks 300% physical damage.
  • Dispelling Null Corona. The remaining absorb jumps to another player — just heal through it.
  • Skipping feathers on Heroic. You cannot outrun Devouring Cosmos without one on most classes.
  • Moving during the 300% arrow damage window in Intermission 2.

Mechanic glossary
Void Barrage
Cast by Undying Voidspawn adds. At 100 energy the add goes unkillable and the cast deals massive raid damage.
How to handle: Assign 3 named interrupters per add with a backup. Never let the cast complete.
Voidstalker Sting
25-second Void DoT on random targets.
How to handle: Healable with standard healing on Heroic. If it's killing people, the real problem is a concurrent mechanic.
Void Expulsion
Ranged-baited puddle dropped on target location.
How to handle: All ranged bait together on the fissure edge, slightly outside melee stack.
Void Remnants
Ground puddles dropped by adds.
How to handle: Drop on the edge of the room. Do not stand in old puddles.
Cosmic Barrier
Large absorb on the Rift Simulacrum with raid-wide ambient damage for the duration.
How to handle: Healers pre-assign a raid CD for each Barrier window. DPS burn the shield.
Silverstrike Ricochet
Bouncing arrow that clears space along the fissure.
How to handle: Rotate counter-clockwise along the fissure edge as space shrinks.
Orbiting Matter
Bolts that fire through assigned platform slices during Intermission 2.
How to handle: Stay centered in your assigned slice. Do not move during the 300% arrow damage debuff.
Aspect of the End
Tethers tank, melee, and ranged players to obelisks. Break order matters.
How to handle: Break order: Ranged → Melee → Tank, 30+ yards apart. Each break stacks 300% physical damage.
Gravity Collapse
Raid damage fired when Aspect tethers break.
How to handle: Survivable with reasonable spacing and correct break order. Cascades if debuffs stack.
Grasp of Emptiness
Obelisk anchor for 8 seconds, then beams fire at expiration.
How to handle: Reposition the group as a unit before the timer expires.
Devouring Cosmos
Covers the current platform slice in a no-heal puddle.
How to handle: Jump to a fresh slice via feather before it finishes spawning. Feathers are required on Heroic.
Bursting Emptiness
Damage when Grasp of Emptiness expires.
How to handle: Same dodge as Grasp — reposition as a unit before expiration.
Dark Rush
Cosmic feather pickup granting a 12-second glide.
How to handle: Required to cross between slices on Heroic. Grab one per Devouring Cosmos cycle.
Null Corona
Heal absorb on a player.
How to handle: Do NOT dispel. Dispelling jumps the remaining absorb to another player. Heal through it.
