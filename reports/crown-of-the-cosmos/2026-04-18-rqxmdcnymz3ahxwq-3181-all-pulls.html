Crown of the Cosmos · Heroic

Mythic+ Season 1 2026-04-18 10 wipes Best: 10.5% fight remaining · boss at 37.7% HP Avg wipe: 5:29
Source: https://www.warcraftlogs.com/reports/RqxmdCnyMZ3AhXWQ

Summary
  • Scope: 10 Phase 2+ progression pulls (fights 8, 9, 17, 18, 21, 22, 23, 24, 26, 28). Best attempt was pull 10, which reached Phase 3 with 37.67% boss HP remaining.
  • Void Barrage is the biggest killer — 57 of 217 deaths. On pull 8, 18 of 21 deaths came from Void Barrage inside 20 seconds. That cast must get interrupted every time.
  • Voidstalker Sting shows up as the top killing blow on paper (53 deaths) but it is not the actual cause. Sting is healable on its own — it finishes players who were already in trouble from a prior mechanic.
  • Ranged puddle drops (Void Expulsion) are inconsistent. Mcnuggs, Tunasat, and Zoeìe each ate it twice. Small per pull, compounds badly when it overlaps with a Barrage.
  • Phase 3 was reached once (pull 10). Five near-simultaneous deaths at ~3:17 point to an out-of-order tether break cascade — Ranged → Melee → Tank order was not held.

Cause mix
Voidstalker Sting 61 deaths
Rarely the real cause. The mechanics guide is explicit: Sting is healable on Heroic. It ticks alongside whatever mechanic actually broke the raid (usually Void Barrage or Cosmic Barrier) and players die on the next Sting tick. Look 5-10 seconds before the Sting death to find the actual mistake.
Void Barrage 57 deaths
Shows up in nearly every P2 wipe. Raid damage spikes once the cast completes and healers collapse within 5-10 seconds. Fight 24 (pull 8) was pure Void Barrage — 18 of 21 deaths from it inside 20 seconds. This is the single biggest killer in the session.
Melee 35 deaths
Concentrated on tanks Diesl (9) and Skidouche (7). They are dying after healers are dead — this is the tail of a raid wipe, not a tank swap issue. Don't scapegoat the tanks.
Void Expulsion 14 deaths
Ranged puddle drops are inconsistent. Mcnuggs, Tunasat, and Zoeìe each ate it twice. That's a specific ranged group not moving out cleanly for the bait.
Cosmic Barrier 12 deaths
Rift ambient damage killing players during the absorb window. Either no healer CD is covering, or Barrier is overlapping directly with a Void Barrage cast.
Void Remnants 10 deaths
Ground puddles. Scattered victims with no single repeat offender — raid-wide placement is loose rather than one player's problem.
Gravity Collapse 5 deaths

Rift Slash 4 deaths

Silverstrike Ricochet 4 deaths

Stellar Emission 3 deaths
