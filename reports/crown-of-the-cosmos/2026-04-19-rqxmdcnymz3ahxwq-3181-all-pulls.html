Summary
  • Analysis limited to pull 24 (fight 28) — the deepest attempt of the night at 10.54% fight remaining with boss at 37.67% HP.
  • Phase 3 fell apart at 8:01 when Gravity Collapse hit the raid for 171k unmitigated, killing 4 people in 3 seconds — that cascade was the wipe.
  • After the Phase 3 cascade, Voidstalker Sting DoT killed 7 more players between 8:30 and 8:38 because the surviving healers could not keep up with the stack of raid damage.
  • Intermission 2 (Orbiting Matter) also cost 3 deaths between 6:38 and 6:56 — damage was taken during movement, which drained healer mana before Phase 3 even started.
  • The final wipe at 9:01-9:02 was both tanks and a healer going down to Grasp of Emptiness beams and Devouring Cosmos ground — the raid did not reposition off the obelisks or clear the slice in time.

Simple strategy

Crown of the Cosmos Heroic is a three-phase Voidspire encounter. Phase 1 is an add wave with Void Barrage interrupts. Intermission 1 / Phase 2 (Severed Rift) is a burn on the Rift Simulacrum with raid-wide Cosmic Barrier damage. Phase 3 (The End of the End) starts at 62% boss HP after the Simulacrum dies and is where the fight actually happens — tether breaks, obelisk beams, and a rotating no-heal floor.

Fight plan

  • Phase 1 (100% → ~85%): Interrupt every Void Barrage. Drop Void Remnants on the edge. Kill Sentinels with Silverstrike arrows, never hit the same Sentinel twice.
  • Intermission 1 / Phase 2: Burn the Rift Simulacrum through Cosmic Barrier. Ranged drops Void Expulsion puddles on the fissure edge. Rotate counter-clockwise on ricochet Silverstrike.
  • Intermission 2 / Orbiting Matter: Rotate slice as a group to dodge bolts. Stay centered during the 300% arrow damage debuff window.
  • Phase 3 (from 62%): Break Aspect of the End tethers from 30+ yards away in order Ranged → Melee → Tank. Step off Grasp of Emptiness beams as a unit. Feather off the Devouring Cosmos slice every cycle. Do not dispel Null Corona.

Phase plan

Phase 1 (100% → ~85%)

Clear the Voidspawn waves with interrupts and use Silverstrike arrows to kill Sentinels cleanly.

Positioning: Melee stacked on the boss, ranged semi-circle behind at 20 yards. Edge of the platform for Void Remnants drops.

Assignments: Three-person interrupt rotation on Void Barrage — missed interrupts scale the add wave hard. Dedicated Silverstrike caller: rotate Sentinel targets so no Sentinel is hit twice.

Movement: Walk out to edge when tagged with Void Remnants. Healers cover Voidstalker Sting DoT with normal single-target heals.

Intermission 1 / Phase 2 — Severed Rift

Burn the Rift Simulacrum while eating Cosmic Barrier raid damage. Keep ranged puddles clean.

Positioning: Raid loose on the fissure, ranged slightly outside melee stack. Tanks hold the Simulacrum on the middle slice.

Assignments: Raid CD rotation commits through Cosmic Barrier. Ranged puddle partners — drop Void Expulsion puddles in pairs on the fissure edge.

Movement: Counter-clockwise rotation on ricochet Silverstrike arrows. Do not cross the fissure — the puddle walk is one-way.

Intermission 2 — Orbiting Matter

Rotate slice to dodge bolts. Stay centered during the arrow debuff window.

Positioning: Each player has a slice anchor point. Centered during the 300% arrow damage debuff.

Assignments: Pick a lead who calls the rotation direction (CW or CCW) for the whole group. Everyone mirrors the lead — no individual rotation decisions.

Movement: Shift slice on the bolt cast, not on the impact. Don't use Burning Rush / movement speed that damages you — you need full health into Phase 3.

Phase 3 — The End of the End (from 62%)

Break tethers correctly, dodge obelisk beams, and feather off the Devouring Cosmos slice every cycle.

Positioning: Spread 30+ yards from your obelisk before breaking Aspect of the End. Tight group movement off Grasp of Emptiness beam lines.

Assignments: Tether break order: Ranged, then Melee, then Tank. Feather pickup assigned per slice — tanks must have a feather every Devouring Cosmos. Do NOT dispel Null Corona — heal it off.

Movement: Step off obelisk beam as a unit at 6 seconds on Grasp. Jump slices before Devouring Cosmos covers the current one — feathers are required on Heroic.

Big mistakes to avoid

  • Breaking Aspect of the End tethers too close or in the wrong order — this stacks the 300% physical damage debuff and makes Gravity Collapse one-shot the raid.
  • Standing in Devouring Cosmos because you did not pick up a feather.
  • Dispelling Null Corona — it jumps the remaining heal absorb to another player.
  • Staying in the Grasp of Emptiness beam line past the 6-second expire.
  • Missing Void Barrage interrupts in Phase 1-2 and letting the add wave snowball.
  • Dropping Void Remnants in the melee stack during Cosmic Barrier (this is exactly what killed Lóveless at 4:44).

How your log differed

  • Phase 1 looked fine — no deaths before 4:44. Interrupts were not flagged as a problem in this pull.
  • Phase 2 had one mechanic failure: Lóveless dropped a Void Remnants puddle where it stacked with Cosmic Barrier and died. One lone death, not a pattern.
  • Intermission 2 broke down — 3 non-healer deaths to Orbiting Matter in 18 seconds (6:38-6:56). This is exactly what the strategy says to avoid and was a group-rotation failure.
  • Phase 3 tethers were the wipe. At 8:01-8:04 four players ate a 171k unmitigated Gravity Collapse — identical damage numbers means the same tether break was wrong for all of them. This is the top priority to fix.
  • Voidstalker Sting killed 7 players between 8:30 and 8:38 not because Sting was handled badly, but because the healer line collapsed in the Gravity Collapse cascade. Fix the tether phase and the Sting wave disappears.
  • Final deaths at 9:01-9:02 (both tanks + a healer) came from Grasp of Emptiness beams and Devouring Cosmos. Tanks specifically need a movement plan — Diesl had Dark Rush but consumed it too late.

Mechanic glossary
Aspect of the End (tethers)
Tethers tank, melee, and ranged players to obelisks. Break order and distance matter.
How to handle: Break from 30+ yards in order Ranged → Melee → Tank. Do not pull early.
Gravity Collapse
Raid-wide damage on tether break. Stacks 300% physical damage taken per break.
How to handle: Break tethers correctly and spread — don't stand on the debuff stacks.
Grasp of Emptiness
Obelisk anchor for 8 seconds, then fires beams on expiration.
How to handle: Move off the beam line as a unit at the 6-second mark.
Bursting Emptiness
Backlash damage when Grasp of Emptiness expires.
How to handle: Clear the beam line before expire — same positioning rule as Grasp.
Dark Rush (cosmic feathers)
Pickup gives a 12-second glide — required to cross slices.
How to handle: Grab a feather every Devouring Cosmos and jump slice on time. Tanks must have one.
Devouring Cosmos
Covers the current slice in a no-heal puddle — primary Phase 3 tempo mechanic.
How to handle: Feather or movement CD to the next slice before it finishes spawning.
Voidstalker Sting
25-second Void DoT on random targets. On Heroic, meant to be healed through.
How to handle: Standard healing. If Sting is killing people, the real problem is elsewhere.
Void Remnants
Ground puddles dropped by Voidspawn adds.
How to handle: Drop on the edge, not in the melee stack.
Cosmic Barrier
Rift Simulacrum absorb shield plus raid-wide ambient damage during Severed Rift.
How to handle: Burn the absorb, raid CD through the ambient damage.
Void Expulsion
Ranged-baited puddle during Severed Rift.
How to handle: Ranged drops puddles together on the fissure edge.
Orbiting Matter
Bolts fire across the platform during intermission 2.
How to handle: Rotate slice as a unit; pick one direction and commit.
Void Barrage
Cast by Undying Voidspawn — lets adds go unkillable if it reaches 100 energy.
How to handle: Three-person interrupt rotation. No misses allowed.
Null Corona
Large heal absorb on a player — dispel jumps the rest to someone else.
How to handle: Do NOT dispel. Heal through it.
